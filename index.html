about & us

All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, also known as AIIMS Delhi, is a public medical research university and hospital in New Delhi, India. The institute is governed by the AIIMS Act, 1956 and operates autonomously under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.


Our Services

Emergency room services

Emergency provide immediate medical care for acute conditions. They address injuries, severe illnesses, and urgent medical needs promptly.

X-Ray

X-rays are used in medical imaging to visualize internal structures. Dense tissues like bones appear white, aiding diagnosis and treatment.

Bones

Bone services include X-rays for fractures, DEXA scans for osteoporosis, CT scans for detailed imaging, MRIs for soft tissue evaluation, and bone scintigraphy for identifying abnormalities like infections or tumors.

medical truck

A nonprofit deploys a medical truck to rural areas, offering check-ups, diagnostics, pharmacy services, and telemedicine to improve healthcare accessibility.

Heart

Heart services include ECG for rhythm, echocardiogram for structure, stress tests for exertion response, catheterization for intervention, and nuclear studies for blood flow assessment.
